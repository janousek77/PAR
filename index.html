Find A Pet To Adopt

It is estimated that 6.5 million companion
animals enter U.S. shelters nationwide every year.


The mission and vision of Project Animal Rescue (PAR) is to help find forever homes by assisting the efforts of Seattle's local animal shelters by displaying the dogs and cats that are currently up for adoption.

We produced this project in an attempt to support the need to adopt rescue animals, but also in that society will one day view companion animals not as disposable commodities, but as a part of the family.

The key principles of our project:

  1. Strive for a clean design that is user-friendly and attractive.
  2. Design a user-friendly interface allowing the user to navigate easily and find the information provided efficiently.
  3. Increase the functionality of the site with addition JavaScript and CSS.
  4. Create a site that has a target audience and help direct users to the appropriate sites.

Navigating our site:

  • Admire the homepage.
  • Read the About Us page to learn about the developers.
  • Search for the type of pet you're looking for on the left hand side of the homepage.
  • Navigate the Dog and Cat adoption pages and select your future companion.
  • Click on your future companion's image. This will redirect you to the Adoption Form where you can input your information.
  • Once your information has been submitted, it will be stored within local storage.
  • Check out the rest of the pages! The Donation page will redirect you to the donations pages of your local Seattle animal shelters!
