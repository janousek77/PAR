Find A Pet To Adopt

It is estimated that 6.5 million companion
animals enter U.S. shelters nationwide every year.


The mission and vision of Project Animal Rescue (PAR) is to help find forever homes by assisting the effort of Seattle's local animal shelters by displaying the dogs and cats that are currently up for adoption.
