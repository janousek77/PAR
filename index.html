Find A Pet To Adopt

It is estimated that 6.5 million companion
animals enter U.S. shelters nationwide every year.


The mission and vision of Project Animal Rescue (PAR) is to help find forever homes by assisting the efforts of Seattle's local animal shelters by displaying the dogs and cats that are currently up for adoption.

We produced this project in an attempt to support the need to adopt rescue animals, but also in that society will one day view companion animals not disposable commodities, but as a part of the family
